Research

I am currently working with Prof. Daniel Cooney.

I am currently exploring research questions related to BioMath. I am particularly interested in analyzing partial differential equations (PDEs) that arise in biomath models, such as reaction-diffusion systems and the public goods dilemma. In addition, I have a strong interest in numerical simulation methods for solving PDEs, including finite difference schemes and related computational techniques. Although I am still in the early stages of my PhD journey, I am eager to develop innovative approaches and deepen my expertise in this area.

Stay tuned for updates on my research progress!
